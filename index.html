05 — Usage

A few rules
worth keeping.

Simple usage standards to keep the brand clean, consistent, and easy to apply across every touchpoint.

Do this
  • Use approved logo versions only
  • Keep clear space around the logo
  • Use the primary color as the dominant color
  • Use accent colors sparingly
  • Use approved fonts or recommended presentation fonts
  • Use the icon mark for small applications
Not this
  • Do not stretch, rotate, or distort the logo
  • Do not place the logo on busy or low-contrast backgrounds
  • Do not use colors outside the approved palette
  • Do not add shadows, gradients, or effects to the logo
  • Do not mix in random fonts
  • Do not rebuild the logo from scratch
